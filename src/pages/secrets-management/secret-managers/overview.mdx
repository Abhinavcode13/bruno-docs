# Integration with Secret managers <PremiumBadge />

For users of Bruno Ultimate, you can take your secret management one step further and perform an integration with a secret vault. 

Currently, we support integrations with:

- [HashiCorp vault](secret-managers/hashicorp-vault)
- [AWS Secrets Manager](secret-managers/aws-secrets-manager)
- [Azure Key Vault](secret-managers/azure-key-vault)
